The problem we solve

Manual outbound is broken. AI is the fix.

The average B2B sales rep spends more than 60% of their working day on tasks that do not require human judgment — researching accounts, writing templated emails, logging CRM updates, and chasing replies. A-Gent replaces that manual layer with autonomous AI agents that run 24/7, so your team focuses on conversations that close.

⌁

The research problem

Reps spend 30–60 minutes researching a single account before first contact. A-Gent's research agents do this in seconds — scanning news, funding events, tech stack signals, and contact data to produce a complete account brief automatically.

✦

The personalization problem

Generic outreach gets ignored. Truly personalized outreach at scale is impossible manually. A-Gent generates messages from your ICP rules, account evidence, pain points, and proof — every message is specific, not templated.

✓

The control problem

Fully autonomous outreach creates brand risk. A-Gent keeps humans in the loop through a review queue — every message is approved before it sends, so quality and brand control stay intact while speed increases dramatically.
